# Sources: Demographics & Adoption Trends

## Primary Research Reports

### Pew Research: Teens, Social Media and AI Chatbots 2025
- **URL**: https://www.pewresearch.com/internet/2025/12/09/teens-social-media-and-ai-chatbots-2025/
- **Published**: December 9, 2025
- **Sample**: 1,458 U.S. teens (September 25 - October 9, 2025)
- **Key Data**: 64% use AI chatbots, 28% daily, race/ethnicity breakdown, age segmentation
- **Significance**: Most comprehensive teen AI usage study to date

### OpenAI ChatGPT Usage Study
- **URL**: https://openai.com/index/how-people-are-using-chatgpt/
- **PDF**: https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/a253471f-8260-40c6-a2cc-aa93fe9f142e/economic-research-chatgpt-usage-paper.pdf
- **Researchers**: OpenAI + Harvard economist David Deming
- **Data**: 1.5 million conversations
- **Key Demographics**: 52.4% female, 46% ages 18-25, income-based growth patterns

### Anthropic Education Report
- **URL**: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropic-education-report-how-university-students-use-claude
- **Published**: April 2025
- **Data**: 1 million anonymized student conversations
- **Key Finding**: CS students 6.8x overrepresented (36.8% of conversations, 5.4% of degrees)

## Global Inequality & Adoption Gap Research

### IMF Working Paper: AI Adoption and Inequality
- **URL**: https://www.imf.org/en/publications/wp/issues/2025/04/04/ai-adoption-and-inequality-565729
- **Published**: April 4, 2025
- **Key Findings**:
  - Global North: 23% adoption
  - Global South: 13% adoption
  - AI growth impact in advanced economies >2x that of low-income countries

### OECD-Cisco: Geographical and Generational Divides
- **URL**: https://www.prnewswire.com/news-releases/oecd-cisco-research-finds-stark-geographical-and-generational-divides-in-ai-uptake-and-digital-well-being-302632418.html
- **Key Findings**:
  - Stark generational divide (under-35s vs older adults)
  - 25% of Asia-Pacific offline
  - Women in South Asia 40% less likely to own smartphone

### UN AI Inequality Warning (Euronews)
- **URL**: https://www.euronews.com/next/2025/12/02/ai-race-is-faster-than-countries-can-adapt-threatening-greater-global-inequality-un-report
- **Published**: December 2, 2025
- **Warning**: AI adoption happening in months, risk of reversing development progress

### Modern Diplomacy: Global South AI Adoption
- **URL**: https://moderndiplomacy.eu/2025/03/04/why-is-ai-adoption-slower-in-the-global-south-and-how-can-it-leap-forward/

- **Key Finding**: India, Brazil, Mexico, South Africa have highest adoption rates
- **Paradox**: Growth fastest in emerging economies, but absolute usage still lower

### Anthropic Economic Index - Geographic Inequality
